Benefits of register company with Lobusch

Lobusch is the only service that provides the opportunity to register a Ukrainian company online.

We have automated most of the processes to enable our customers to register a company
as quickly as possible. To register a company with a nominee director, you need to spend only 5-10 minutes filling out the form. With other registration options, you will need to additionally
send a power of attorney to our legal address.
